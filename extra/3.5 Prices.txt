To subtract 3.5% from price: price - (price * 3.5%)

Which is: price - (price * 0.035)

Factor out price: price * (1 - 0.035)

Which gives: price * 0.965

Query tool on the database : database.schema.table

-- Update price by subtracting 3.5% (multiplying by 0.965)
UPDATE your_table_name
SET price = ROUND(price * 0.965, 4);
